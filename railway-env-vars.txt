Copy these environment variables to Railway:

DJANGO_SECRET_KEY=django-insecure-your-super-secret-key-change-this-to-something-random-and-long
DJANGO_DEBUG=0
DJANGO_ALLOWED_HOSTS=*.railway.app
GROQ_API_KEY=your-groq-api-key-here
CORS_ALLOWED_ORIGINS=https://your-vercel-app.vercel.app
CORS_ALLOW_ALL=0
CSRF_TRUSTED_ORIGINS=https://*.railway.app,https://your-vercel-app.vercel.app
LOG_LEVEL=INFO

Instructions:
1. In Railway dashboard, go to your project
2. Click "Variables" tab
3. Add each variable above (one by one)
4. Replace "your-groq-api-key-here" with your actual Groq API key
5. Replace "your-vercel-app.vercel.app" with your actual Vercel domain
6. Railway will automatically provide DATABASE_URL for PostgreSQL
